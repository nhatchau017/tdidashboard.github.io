Untire

Tired of Cancer Foundation · Netherlands
Most Similar to the aim of current project Cancer-specific

12-week cancer-related fatigue self-management program structured around 9 themes: energy, sleep, stress, fear, fatigue, battery, food, and movement. Combines psychoeducation, CBT, mindfulness-based stress reduction, and graded physical activity.

Wysa

Wysa Inc. · USA / India
Include AI chatbot features AI-mediated

AI-powered mental wellness chatbot featuring a penguin mascot, conversational CBT, guided exercises for anxiety and depression, automatic journaling, goal-setting planner, and mood tracking. Anonymous onboarding with optional therapist escalation. Independently verified for 5 crisis support pathways.

MindShift CBT

Anxiety Canada · Canada
Developed by CAMH and Anxiety Canada Self-guided

Free CBT-based anxiety management app with 1.5M+ lifetime downloads. Featured mood check-in tracker (MS Face slider), thought journal, fear ladder, guided breathing and meditation (Chill Zone), coping cards, and a moderated community forum.

MindSpot

MQ Health / Macquarie University · Australia
Based on iCanADAPT in Perri’s review Therapist-guided

Online psychological treatment service offering structured therapist-guided CBT programs for depression, anxiety, PTSD, and chronic pain. Validated assessments at intake (PHQ-9, GAD-7, K-10). Asynchronous therapist messaging.

Clear Fear

stem4 · United Kingdom
Match eligibility age range Youth co-design

CBT-based anxiety app for adolescent and young people aged 16–25. Features organised around the FEAR acronym (Feel it, Explore it, Accept it, Release it). Includes breathing exercises, worry box, fear exposure ladder, safety net planning, and the Glide gamification mini-game.
